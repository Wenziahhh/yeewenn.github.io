Challenges

  • Lack of Access in Rural and Remote Areas:
    -Many children in rural or underdeveloped areas do not have access to nearby schools.
    -Many distances and poor infrastructure often make attendance difficult.

  • Gender Inequality in Education:
    -Girls in some cultures face barriers to education due to traditional norms and early marriages.
    - Discrimination limits their access to learning opportunities.

  • Economic Barriers:
    -Families living in poverty may prioritize work over education for their children.
    -Hidden costs like uniforms, transportation, and supplies make schooling inaccessible.

  • Poor Infrastructure and Learning Environments:
    -Many schools lack basic facilities like clean water, electricity, and proper classroom spaces, affecting the learning experience.
